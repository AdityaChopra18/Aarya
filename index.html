About Aksharanjali

Welcome to Aksharanjali 2025, the premier literary festival celebrating the art of words and ideas. Drawing inspiration from the great thinkers of India , our festival brings together students, faculty, and literary enthusiasts for two days of creative expression and literary competition.

This year, we're focusing on three prestigious competitions that showcase the best literary talent from across the colleges in Ajmer.
